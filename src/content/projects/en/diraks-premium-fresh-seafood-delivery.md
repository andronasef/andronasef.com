## Project Overview

Diraks is a specialty seafood brand delivering fresh, high-quality fish from Jazan's rich waters straight to customers' doors. With a focus on authenticity, premium packaging, and same-day delivery, Diraks brings the best of Jazan's maritime heritage to modern seafood lovers.

To enhance its online presence and streamline the shopping experience, I developed a Salla-based e-commerce website that reflects the brand's identity, ensures effortless ordering, and highlights its unique value propositions.

## Project Objective

The goal was to design a visually appealing, easy-to-use, high-converting e-commerce platform that:

- Showcases Diraks' premium seafood selection.
- Emphasizes freshness, quality, and same-day delivery.
- Provides a seamless shopping and checkout experience.

## Key Features

- **Modern & responsive design** — A sleek, mobile-friendly interface for smooth browsing on all devices.
- **Well-organized product catalog** — High-quality images, clear descriptions, and structured categories.
- **Fast & simple ordering** — Direct WhatsApp ordering integration for instant interaction.
- **Same-day delivery assurance** — A prominent feature highlighting freshness and speed.
- **Authentic branding & storytelling** — A design inspired by Jazan's maritime heritage.
- **Flexible payment options** — Cash on delivery, installment plans (Tabby, Tamara), and online payments.
- **Customer reviews & social integration** — Real feedback plus links to Snapchat, TikTok, and Instagram.

## Benefits

- **Stronger online presence** — A professional platform establishing Diraks as a leading seafood brand.
- **Enhanced customer experience** — A fast, engaging process that converts visitors into loyal buyers.
- **Increased sales & growth potential** — A scalable site ready to expand into new cities and services.
- **Brand loyalty & recognition** — Authentic storytelling and premium presentation that differentiate Diraks.

Diraks blends tradition with convenience, offering seafood lovers an unmatched experience of Jazan's finest catches — delivered fresh, clean, and ready to enjoy.
